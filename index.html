Overview

Uzer allows homeowners, renters, landlords and regular people to share their wireless/data network and provide Internet access to numerous paying customers. It's basically wireless made accessible by everyone to everyone.

Unlocked features

Through Uzer, the owner of the router will share their wireless network connection in return for money. The owner can set up parameters for the user in order to ensure safety, such as the length of use and the amount of data allowed for use.

Benefits

With Uzer, people who travel abroad
will no longer have to pay ridiculously expensive roaming fees to use Internet. Owners can cut back on their monthly wireless & data costs by putting the money earned through Uzer towards their Internet bills and potentially generating extra income for them.

Ratings system

Our rating system allows the owners to get the best possible feedbacks about their Internet usage and state while making sure to indicate to users which providers offer the strongest and fastest possible connection allowing the app users to feel more at ease and safer towards the usage of the Uzer app.
