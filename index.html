Overview

Uzer allows homeowners, tenents, landlords and businesses to share their wireless/data network and sell Internet access to the public. Uzer is wireless made accessible by everyone for everyone.

Uzers

Uzer provides unlimited Wi-Fi for a cheap rate. People who travel abroad will no longer have to pay ridiculously expensive roaming fees to use Internet. Light internet users will no longer have to pay for monthly internet. And students can use UZER instead of LTE Data and save on their monthly phone bills.

Uzer providers

Inernet access at home can be expensive. Uzer can help with that! You are already paying for internet so why not get compensatd for sharing it. Uzer can help you cut back on your monthly Internet bills and potentially profit every month.


Pricing

Choose the right option for you.

Pay per minute

Uzer offers two payment options for using Wi-Fi. The pay per minute model offers unlimited Wi-Fi within the purched time. This option is good for professionals who spend lengthy amounts of time on the internet downloading and viewing files.

Pay per megabyte

The pay per megabyte is for casual Internet browsers that would like to pay only for the internet they use up. Uzer employs algorithms that determine exact rates per megabyte depending on speed and quality of the connections.
