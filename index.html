how is our work

Process of Creation

  • internet security
  • user experience
  • design direction
  • software development

We have a team of well-rounded offensive security experts who conduct security assessments every day on the Uzer app. We make sure that every transaction is back-to-end encrypted and that no customer information is ever leaked to anyone other than us. That's our top priority and Uzer won't and can't live without it.

We're constantly meeting with application and information security experts who demonstrate unique capabilities in malware design, reverse engineering, penetration testing and who have taken part in a wide range of cybercrime prevention consulting whilst being actively involved in the information gathering (OSINT), attack\defence patterns design through to planning and eventual execution.

We believe in the simplicity of User Experience. That is essentially the driving force we believe will give us a rapid success. Uzer is a cloud-based technology that will use a clever UX strategy to disrupt an entire industry.

We're calling for Wifi that is easier, more convenient and more secure using a clever interface and security measures to turn the process of calling Wifi into something not unlike playing a game app.

By the careful melding of visual design, information architecture and usability, the Uzer App’s UX will create a welcoming and intuitive experience that evokes feelings of delight, curiosity and satisfaction - just like a masterpiece.

Many people will tell you that ‘content is king and design is queen’, but here, we think neither the inverse nor the opposite - it’s the user that is king and queen - and the design and content is centered around you and your whole experience. It's meant just for you.

While we want Uzer’s UI to be simple, we engineer complex systems behind it to stay up, handle difficult calculations, and serve massive amounts of demands. We get to divide the originally simple building into many categories to stretch and fill the needs.

Unlike typical tech services, Uzer has only transactional users: internet users and providers. Those people rely on our technology to get connected or make some extra money, so there’s no safe time to make a mistake. Our software is hence based on finding availability and measuring distances asap.
